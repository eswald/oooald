#21.    The 11th March Christened the uægt* male child
called Ole, who's father became known to be the married man
Iver Olsen Lille Moe, huusmand under Valle, and mother Guro
Olsdatter Qvalshoug, confirmed of Strands Parish and called this;
who remains at the home of Johne Hunsness... _ Witnesses:
Johne Hunsness, Sjur Olsen Qualshoug, Jacob Pedersen Solbiör;
Torbör Hellen under Sande, Britha Pedersdatter Hjelmelandsvaagen.
(*uægt : The parents were unmarried when the child was born.  There
is a second copy of this record on page 335 #5 in the original book.)
